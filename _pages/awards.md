# Awards and Recognition

These honors recognize excellence in teaching, pedagogical innovation, and contributions to the mathematics community at national, regional, and institutional levels.

## Recent Awards

* 2026 [Deborah and Franklin Tepper Haimo Award for Distinguished College or University Teaching of Mathematics](https://maa.org/deborah-and-franklin-tepper-haimo-award-for-distinguished-college-or-university-teaching-of-mathematics/), Mathematical Association of America.
* 2025 [John M. Smith Award, Mathematical Association of America](http://sections.maa.org/mddcva/smith_award.php), Mathematical Association of America, MD-DC-VA Section.
* 2024 [Faculty Excellence Award in Teaching](https://cnu.edu/academics/provosts-office/programs/), Christopher Newport University.
