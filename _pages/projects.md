RESEARCH & THEORY

## [Cupolets and Chaotic Entanglement](https://github.com/matthew-morena/cupolets)

Python implementation of periodic orbit extraction and visualization for coupled chaotic systems. Includes numerical solvers, entropy calculations, and visualization of entangled cupolets in systems ranging from simple coupled oscillators to computational neuroscience models.

**Relevance:** research, reproducible — demonstrates dynamical systems theory applied to chaos, with clean numerical methods and literate code.

PEDAGOGICAL MATERIALS
===================

## [AI and Machine Learning Modules for Undergraduate Mathematics](https://github.com/matthew-morena/math-ai-ml-modules)

Self-contained Jupyter notebooks and guided activities integrating AI/ML literacy into calculus, linear algebra, differential equations, and mathematical modeling. Each module connects mathematical reasoning to real-world computational applications, with optional instructor solutions and ethical reflection prompts.

**Relevance:** pedagogical, reproducible, public-facing — designed for broad adoption across mathematics curricula and easily adapted for other institutions.

## [HONR 310: Fractals and Infinity Course Materials](https://github.com/matthew-morena/fractals-and-infinity)

Jupyter labs, Python visualizations, and problem sets for an honors seminar bridging classical fractal theory and computational exploration. Includes interactive Julia set generators, iterated function systems, and dimension calculations.

**Relevance:** pedagogical, reproducible — complete course scaffold for teaching fractals with hands-on numerical experimentation.

APPLIED DATA ANALYSIS
====================

## [Water Quality Data Analysis: Lynnhaven River NOW](https://github.com/matthew-morena/lynnhaven-river-data)

Exploratory analysis and visualization of water quality metrics from Lynnhaven River NOW's ongoing monitoring program. Includes time-series analysis, statistical summaries, and interactive dashboards for identifying trends and anomalies in environmental data.

**Relevance:** applied research, community-engaged, reproducible — demonstrates translation of environmental science into accessible data storytelling for nonprofit stakeholders.
